# Social / LinkedIn Post Format

Write a single short social announcement as Markdown:

1. A one-line hook naming the most user-visible approved change.
2. Two or three sentences of plain-language detail — what users can now do and why
   it matters. Lead with user value, not implementation detail.
3. An optional short bulleted list (max three items) if multiple features ship.
4. Close with a neutral, non-promotional line. No call-to-action promising
   unreleased work.

Keep it under ~120 words. No hashtags spam (at most two), no emoji walls, no
fabricated metrics, customer names, or availability dates.
