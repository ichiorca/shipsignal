# Audience Map

Map each artifact to the audience that will read it and frame the value for them.

- Blog/changelog: existing users and evaluators. Lead with what they can now do and how
  to start; assume familiarity with the product, not with this release.
- Sales one-pager: prospects and the buyer. Lead with the business outcome and the
  problem solved; keep implementation detail out.
- Social snippets: a broad, skimming audience. One concrete benefit per post, no jargon.
- Demo script / audio digest: a viewer following along. Narrate the user's path, not the
  system's internals.
- Match every claim to what the audience can verify from the released product; never
  promise roadmap, pricing, or performance the evidence does not support.
- Take wording cues from [[brand-voice]] and product facts from [[product-context]]; keep
  anything in [[redaction-rules]] out regardless of audience.
